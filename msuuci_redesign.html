"Where students develop into confident, spiritual, moral and high-achieving leaders."

  • Religious Services

    The Muslim Student Union is an organization that uniquely ties its religious goals into every event and activity it hosts. The MSU aims to provide a venue for Muslim students on the UCI campus to practice their religion and to grow spiritually. Encouraging an enhanced spiritual connection, many students find the religious services provided to be the most important aspect provided to them on campus.

  • College Life

    The Muslim Student Union at UC Irvine strives to establish a solid sisterhood and brotherhood between its members by providing a welcoming, safe, and open environment for Muslim students on campus. Each week is filled with several social activities, from sisters' chai nights to brothers' breakfasts to quarterly MSU dinners at local restaurants. Through these activities, MSU provides a home away from home and a support system during the most crucial years of a person's life.

  • Social Justice

    The Muslim Student Union strives to bring to life the Islamic teachings to "enjoin what is right and forbid what is wrong" through the pursuit of social justice for those suffering oppression and exploitation both domestically and internationally. As a vocal and firm advocate for victims of injustice, the Muslim Student Union aims to make strides towards creating awareness of issues regarding social injustice, paving a path for changing attitudes, perspectives, and support.

  • Islam Awareness Month 2014

    The Muslim Student Union at UC Irvine would like to extend a welcome to UCI students, faculty and for all in the community to join us during our annual Islam Awareness Month. Throughout the month we will be presenting and discussing topics regarding the history of Muslims in America, science and education in Islam, coexistence and human rights, and lastly the Muslim experience. Starting Week 4(January 20th) till Week 7 (February 21st) we will be hosting events including a day of fasting and an open house for Friday prayer. Check out the flier below for a full list of events and we hope to see you there, inshaAllah (God willing)!
